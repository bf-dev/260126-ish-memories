Insung created a Self-Diagnosis Bot using NodeJS, Puppeteer Headless Chrome, and Discord.js. The project was initiated during the COVID period to address issues with the official self-diagnosis app, which Insung identified as battery-draining and prone to malfunctions. Insung designed the Discord bot to allow users to complete their daily health checkup by typing the command !imok. Insung ensured the bot respected the purpose of the government policy. Following a government request, Insung took the bot down and complied fully with the request.

Insung developed an application called SimpleNet for CNSA. The technologies used for this project include Python, Selenium, and Google Cloud Run. The project addressed problems with the school portal known as CNSANet, specifically that its messaging system was slow and required repeated logins. Insung's solution was an app that fetches messages from the portal, sends push notifications to users, and serves cached data from the CNSANet portal to improve performance.
